Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data.
Last Updated: 9/5/2025

At Milkar Seva Sansthan, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines our data practices.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect details like your name, email, phone number, and payment info during registration, purchases, or app use.
  • Usage Data: We collect device info, IP address, and activity logs to improve functionality and user experience.
  • Location Data: With permission, we collect your location to offer services like nearest delivery options.

Use of Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To fulfill orders, process payments, and send updates.
  • App Improvement: To analyze feedback and improve app features.
  • Promotional Communications: To send offers and updates (with your consent).
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and stop fraudulent activities.

Information Sharing

We don’t sell your data. We only share it with trusted service providers (like payment gateways and delivery partners) under strict confidentiality agreements.

Data Security and Retention

We protect your data with encryption, firewalls, and secure storage. Your data is retained only as long as needed for orders and legal compliance, after which it is securely deleted.

User Rights

You can request to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can also ask how your data is used.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. All updates will be posted here with the revised “Last Updated” date.
