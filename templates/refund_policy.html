Refund Policy

We value your trust. Learn how refunds are processed with us.

Last Updated: 5 September 2025
Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are applicable only in cases where the product or service delivered is found to be defective, damaged, or not as described. Requests for refunds must be initiated within 7 days of purchase.

Non-Refundable Items
  • Digital products such as eBooks, online courses, and subscriptions
  • Services that have already been availed or partially completed
  • Products purchased during clearance sales or marked as “non-refundable”
Refund Process
  • Request a Refund: Contact our customer support team with proof of purchase and reason for the refund.
  • Verification: Our team will review your request and verify the issue.
  • Processing: Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days to the original payment method.
Refund Timelines

Once approved, the refund amount will be credited within 7–10 business days. Please note that bank or payment gateway delays are beyond our control.
