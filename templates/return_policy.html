Return Policy

Not satisfied with your purchase?
Find out how to return it.

Last Updated: 9/5/2025
Return Eligibility

Items may be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are in original condition and packaging. Please note that digital products, perishable goods, and items marked as "final sale" are not eligible for return.

Non-Returnable Items
  • Digital products, such as ebooks and online subscriptions
  • Perishable goods
  • Items marked as “final sale” or “non-returnable” at the time of purchase
Return Process
  • Initiate Return: Contact our customer support team within 7 days of receiving the item to initiate a return.
  • Prepare the Package: Pack the item securely in its original packaging, including all accessories and documentation.
  • Shipping: Return shipping costs may apply unless the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., incorrect item or damaged product).
Refunds for Returns

Once the returned item is received and inspected, a refund will be processed within 7–10 business days to the original payment method. Refunds are issued after verifying that the returned item meets our eligibility criteria.
